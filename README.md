## 🛠 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the software does not work as expected, refer to this list for fixes.

*   **General Errors:** Restart the application if it freezes. Close all other programs that use your graphics card, such as video games.
*   **No Movement:** Ensure that your graphics drivers are up to date. You can find these on the website of your graphics card manufacturer.
*   **Audio Issues:** Check that your speakers are set as the primary output device in your Windows sound settings.
*   **Performance Lags:** Lower the quality slider in the settings menu if the video looks slow. This reduces pressure on your computer hardware.
